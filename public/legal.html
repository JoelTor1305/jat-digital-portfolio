Sidekick connects to your Notion workspace solely to retrieve contextual information (pages, databases, notes) to assist in completing tasks. This access is read-only or write-only as directed by you.

What We Do With Your Data

  • Notion data is accessed in real time to generate responses — it is not stored on any external server beyond your own VPS.

Data Storage

Any data persisted by Sidekick (e.g., conversation memory, context) is stored on infrastructure you own and control. Sidekick does not maintain independent databases outside your environment.

Third-Party Services

Sidekick may route requests through third-party AI APIs (e.g., Anthropic Claude API) to generate responses. These providers have their own privacy policies. No personally identifiable information is intentionally transmitted beyond what is required to complete your request.

Your Rights

Since you are both the operator and sole user of Sidekick, you have complete control over all data. You may revoke Notion access at any time via your Notion workspace integration settings.

This policy may be updated as Sidekick's functionality evolves. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.
